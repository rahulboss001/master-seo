⚡ Quick Reference — Essential SEO Metrics

Title Tag

50–60 characters. Primary keyword near start. Unique per page. Compelling for clicks.

Meta Description

150–160 characters. Include keyword. Add soft CTA. Improves CTR, not a direct ranking factor.

Page Speed (LCP)

Under 2.5s = Good. 2.5–4s = Needs Improvement. Over 4s = Poor (ranking penalty).

Target KD (New Site)

Under 30 for new domains. Under 50 for DR30+. Under 70 for DR60+.

Content Length

How-to guides: 1,500–3,000 words. Pillar pages: 3,000–8,000. Product pages: 200–500 unique words.

Internal Links

Every page should have at least 2–3 internal links from relevant pages. No orphan pages.

Image Size

Under 100KB per image ideally. Use WebP format. Always include descriptive alt text.

Backlink Quality

DR 60+ = excellent. DR 40–59 = good. DR 20–39 = acceptable for new sites.

Good Keyword Volume

500–5,000/month for new sites. Anything over 100 is worth targeting if KD is low enough.

Local Review Target

Minimum 50 reviews to compete. 4.5+ star average. Respond to every single review.
